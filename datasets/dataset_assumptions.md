# Dataset Assumptions

- The demo uses three Mars harvest modules with distinct crop mixes and risk profiles.
- Seeded fixtures are preferred over fully dynamic simulation where predictability improves demo quality.
- `astro agent` represents astronaut workload, dispatch planning, and human operational impact.
- Water recycling, power availability, and disease suspicion are the highest-value incident levers for the demo.
- Harvest scores are composite demo values, not scientific truth claims.
- Each scenario must visibly alter orchestrator output plus at least two specialist reports.
